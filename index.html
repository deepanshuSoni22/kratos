INTRODUCTION

Born in the brutal lands of ancient Sparta, Kratos was molded by the harsh realities of war and sacrifice. His life, steeped in tragedy and fury, began on the battlefield where honor and pain intermingled. As a relentless warrior, he soon found himself entangled in the divine intrigues of gods and titans, marking the beginning of an epic saga that would redefine the meaning of vengeance and redemption.

STORY

Haunted by the ghosts of his past and betrayed by those he once trusted, Kratos embarks on a personal crusade. His fury is not just a means of survival, but a catalyst for a destiny written in blood and fire.

As the pantheon of gods watches with both fear and awe, Kratos challenges the celestial order. His battles are epic clashes that shake the foundations of myth, as divine powers are forced to reckon with mortal defiance.

FAQ

  • Who exactly is Kratos?

    Kratos is the central character known for his brutal combat style and complex emotional journey. Initially introduced as a fierce and vengeful warrior in ancient Greece, his character grows to face moral dilemmas and personal losses, leading him into new conflicts and self-discovery in later games.

  • Why did Kratos kill Zeus?

    Kratos killed Zeus as the ultimate act of vengeance after being betrayed and manipulated by the gods throughout his tragic journey.

  • Why did Kratos turn against the gods?

    Betrayal, loss, and the manipulation of his past pushed Kratos to reject the divine order and seek retribution against those who wronged him.
